Cookie Policy

What cookies are

"Cookies" is a loose term for small bits of data a website stores in your browser. We use the term broadly here to also cover localStorage, which works similarly. Together they let a site remember things between visits.

What we currently use (essential, always on)

These keep the site working and remember your choices. They are not used for tracking and never leave your device.

  • vd_prefs: your theme (light or dark) and units (metric or imperial). Without this the site forgets your preferences every visit.
  • vd_profiles: profiles you saved inside the app.
  • vd_history: weights you logged manually.
  • vd_consent: your cookie consent choice, so we don't show the banner every time.

What we may use after consent

  • Google AdSense advertising cookies, if and when we enable ads. These set cookies for ad personalization and frequency capping. We will only enable AdSense after you give consent through the banner. You can change your mind any time by clearing the vd_consent value in your browser, which brings the banner back.

Third-party cookies

When parts of the page load from another company (Tailwind CDN, Chart.js CDN, Google Fonts), or when you click an Amazon affiliate link, those companies may set their own cookies under their own privacy policies. We do not control them. The links below open the relevant policies if you want to read them:

  • Google (Fonts, AdSense)
  • Amazon
  • Cloudflare (cdnjs)

How to manage

You can clear cookies and localStorage through your browser settings. Doing so will reset your VitalDash preferences and remove any saved profiles or weight log entries. Step-by-step instructions for the major browsers are in the Privacy Policy.

If you only want to revisit the consent banner, clear the vd_consent value (or all vitaldash.io storage) in your browser's developer tools or settings, then reload the page.

Consent

On your first visit you will see a small banner. You can choose:

  • Essential only: only the storage required for the site to function.
  • Accept all: also allows advertising cookies once we enable them. Today this is identical to "Essential only" because no ads are running yet.

You can change your mind any time. See "How to manage" above.
