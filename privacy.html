Privacy Policy

Short version: we don't run a backend. Your weight, height, age, and goals never leave your browser. The only data anyone has is what you keep on your own device.

Who we are

VitalDash is a free health calculator built and operated by Gaston, a private individual based in Belgium. For privacy questions, this is the data controller you can contact.

What data we collect

None of your inputs are sent to a server we control. VitalDash runs entirely in your browser. We do not have a database. We do not have user accounts.

The site stores small amounts of data locally in your browser (in localStorage) so it can remember settings between visits:

  • vd_prefs: your theme (light or dark) and units (metric or imperial)
  • vd_profiles: profiles you saved inside the app
  • vd_history: weights you logged manually
  • vd_consent: which cookie level you chose

This data stays on your device. We cannot read it. Clearing your browser storage removes it permanently.

Cookies

We use only essential storage today. We do not place tracking cookies. See the Cookie Policy for the details.

Third-party services

Some parts of the site load resources from other companies. When that happens, those companies receive standard browser request data (your IP address, browser type, the page that requested the resource). We do not share anything else with them.

  • Tailwind CDN (cdn.tailwindcss.com): loads our CSS framework.
  • Chart.js CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com): loads the chart library used for the macro and weight charts.
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com): loads the Inter font.
  • Amazon affiliate links: when you click an Amazon link, your browser visits amazon.com. From that point Amazon operates under its own privacy policy. We may receive a small commission if you buy something.
  • Google AdSense (planned, not active yet): if and when we add ads, this section and the cookie banner will be updated. Until then, no AdSense scripts run.

Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • access your data
  • correct it
  • delete it
  • restrict how it is processed
  • get a copy of it (data portability)
  • object to processing
  • file a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Belgium, the Data Protection Authority)

Because we do not hold your data on our servers, you exercise most of these rights yourself by clearing your browser's localStorage. If you have a question or want help, contact us.

How to clear all VitalDash data

  • Chrome / Edge: Settings, then Privacy and security, then Cookies and other site data, then See all site data, search for vitaldash, click Remove.
  • Firefox: Settings, then Privacy and Security, then Cookies and Site Data, click Manage Data, search for vitaldash, click Remove Selected.
  • Safari: Preferences, then Privacy, then Manage Website Data, search for vitaldash, click Remove.

Children

VitalDash is not directed at children under 16. Calorie and weight calculators are not appropriate for children without medical guidance. Parents and guardians should make these decisions with a qualified clinician.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site grows. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version. Material changes will be highlighted in the cookie banner the next time you visit.
